# Backgrounds & Styling

## Background Presets

Frameup includes 45 background presets organized into categories:

### Gradients (25 presets)

Rich gradient backgrounds for marketing visuals:

- **Purples & Blues** — Purple Haze, Violet Dream, Deep Indigo, Ocean Blue, Sky Light, Royal Navy
- **Warm tones** — Warm Sunset, Peach Glow, Golden Hour, Coral Reef, Sunrise, Mango
- **Greens** — Emerald, Forest, Mint
- **Neutrals** — Cool Grey, Slate, Charcoal, Midnight, Dark Ember
- **Vibrant** — Rainbow, Aurora, Cotton Candy, Neon, Synthwave
- **Soft** — Blush Top, Twilight, Dawn, Ice, Lavender

### Solid Colors (14 presets)

Clean single-color backgrounds:

White, Snow, Light Gray, Zinc, Black, Slate, Blue, Indigo, Purple, Pink, Red, Orange, Green, Teal

### Mesh Gradients (5 presets)

Complex multi-point gradient backgrounds:

Cosmos, Nebula, Aqua, Lava, Garden

### Transparent

Export with no background (transparent PNG). Useful for compositing mockups into other designs.

## Device Shadow

Toggle a subtle drop shadow beneath the device frame for added depth. Enable/disable in the Background panel.
