# Free & Open Source

Frameup Free is completely free — no subscription, no account, no limits.

Every feature is available to everyone:

- All 24 device frames
- All 50+ background presets
- Unlimited exports (PNG and WebP)
- Batch export packs (App Store, Play Store, Mac App Store, Social Media)
- Text and badge overlays
- All capture sources (URL, upload, iOS Simulator, Sitemap, Notion, Excel/CSV)
- Projects saved locally

No sign-in required. All data is stored on your machine.
