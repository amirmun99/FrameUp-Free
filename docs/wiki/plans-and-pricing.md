# Plans & Pricing

## Free

Get started with core features at no cost.

- URL and Upload capture
- Select device frames (iPhone 17, Chrome browser)
- 3 background presets
- 10 exports per month
- PNG export

## Pro — $12/month

Everything in Free, plus:

- All 177 device frames
- All 45 background presets + transparent
- Unlimited exports
- Batch export (App Store, Play Store, Social Media packs)
- Text and badge overlays
- iOS Simulator capture
- Sitemap scraper (batch capture)
- Notion page capture
- Excel/CSV capture
- WebP export format

## Lifetime — $149 (one-time)

All Pro features, forever. No recurring payments.

## Managing Your Plan

- View your current plan in the **Account** menu (top-right of the app)
- Upgrade from the **Upgrade** page (accessible from the sidebar)
- Payment is processed securely via Stripe
