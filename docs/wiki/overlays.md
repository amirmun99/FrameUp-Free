# Overlays

Overlays let you add text and badges on top of your mockup. They are positioned on the canvas and can be dragged, resized, and rotated.

## Text Overlays

Add custom text to your mockup for headlines, descriptions, or call-to-action copy.

**Customizable properties:**
- **Text content** — Any text string
- **Font size** — Adjustable size in pixels
- **Font weight** — Light to bold
- **Font family** — System fonts
- **Color** — Any hex color
- **Alignment** — Left, center, or right

## Badge Overlays

Add store badges and promotional badges:

- **App Store** — Apple App Store download badge
- **Play Store** — Google Play Store download badge
- **Mac App Store** — Mac App Store download badge
- **Product Hunt** — Product Hunt badge

Badges are SVG-based for crisp rendering at any size.

## Positioning

- **Drag** overlays to reposition them on the canvas
- **Resize** by dragging the handles
- **Rotate** using the rotation handle
- **Delete** by selecting and pressing Delete/Backspace

## Tips

- Use text overlays for App Store screenshot copy (e.g., "Your productivity, reimagined")
- Combine a headline overlay with a store badge for complete marketing visuals
- Overlays export at full resolution — they scale with the export size
